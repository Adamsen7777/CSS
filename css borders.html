BROCK LESNER

WRESTLER

In 2000, Lesnar signed with the World Wrestling Federation (WWF) and was sent to its developmental territory Ohio Valley Wrestling (OVW), where he first met future friend and manager Paul Heyman.[23] OVW booker Jim Cornette paired Lesnar with his former college roommate Shelton Benjamin in October 2000.[24] They were known as The Minnesota Stretching Crew and won the OVW Southern Tag Team Championship on three separate occasions.[25] Lesnar wrestled several dark matches in 2001 and 2002.
